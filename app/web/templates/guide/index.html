Fast workflows

Use the shortest path for the job.

Water changesPercentage or measured volume

Use a percentage preset, repeat the last change, or enter gallons/liters. Conditioner, nitrate/TDS comparisons, cleaning tasks, duration, and notes are optional.

Water testsEnter only what you tested

Record ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, pH, temperature, and optional advanced values. Previous readings stay visible for quick comparison.

FeedingRecent foods and repeat-last

Select one or several recently used foods, type a new food when needed, repeat the last feeding, or record a planned skipped feeding.

Observations and photosCapture what you notice

Use quick categories for behavior, health, algae, plant growth, water clarity, equipment, or livestock loss. Phone photo logging can open the rear camera directly.

Livestock and plantsAdd, adjust, move, or remove

Choose from the built-in catalog or enter a custom name. Quantity changes, moves, and reasoned removals stay connected to history instead of disappearing.

Common questions

When something does not look right

Why do I not see Plant Care or another feature?

Open Settings and review Feature Modules. Plant Care can be Auto, On, or Off so simple tanks stay quiet.

How do I change gallons, liters, or temperature units?

Open Settings, choose US or Metric, then adjust volume and temperature independently if needed.

Why did AquaOps say care is due?

Open the Care Queue. Schedule reminders and water-based recommendations include an explanation of why they appeared.

Where can I see everything that happened?

Events is the shared chronological history. A tank page narrows the same story to one aquarium, and Reports summarizes patterns.

Can I take my data with me?

Yes. Settings → Download Your Data creates a ZIP containing CSV datasets and a JSON manifest.

How do I switch the color theme?

On a phone, use the sun/moon button in the top bar. On larger screens, choose Light, System, or Dark from the header.
